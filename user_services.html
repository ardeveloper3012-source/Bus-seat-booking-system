User Services

Bus Search and Booking:

Our Bus Search and Booking service allows users to search for available buses between multiple cities. Users can filter results based on departure time, bus type, price range, and seat availability. The booking process is streamlined with real-time seat selection and instant confirmation, ensuring a hassle-free experience for passengers.

Seat Selection:

The Seat Selection feature provides an interactive seat map where users can visually choose their preferred seats. The interface clearly indicates available, booked, and reserved seats, along with gender indicators for privacy preferences. Users can select multiple seats for group bookings and view pricing for each seat category.

Booking History:

Our Booking History service maintains a complete record of all user bookings, including past and upcoming trips. Users can view booking details, download tickets, check booking status, and easily re-book their favorite routes. This feature also allows users to track their travel patterns and manage their travel efficiently.

Profile Management:

Profile Management enables users to maintain their personal information and travel preferences securely. Users can update their contact details, manage notification preferences, add emergency contacts, and save frequently traveled routes for faster booking in the future.
