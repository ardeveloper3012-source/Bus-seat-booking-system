Why Choose Movenco?

Movenco is Pakistan's leading online bus booking platform, connecting travelers with reliable bus services across the country. Our system offers real-time seat availability and instant confirmation, making your travel planning hassle-free. With competitive prices and 24/7 customer support, we ensure a seamless booking experience for every passenger.

Movenco Objective:

The objective of this project is to develop a reliable and user-friendly Bus Seat Booking System that streamlines the process of reserving seats on buses. It aims to eliminate manual booking errors, provide real-time seat availability, and offer administrative control over routes, schedules, and pricing. The system is designed to enhance operational efficiency and improve the user experience for every passenger and administrators.

How to Book Bus seat in Movenco:

  • ➤ Enter the departure and destination locations in the "From" and "To" tab respectively.
  • ➤ Select the "Date of Your Bus Trip" and hit the "Search" button.
  • ➤ Select a bus service of your choice from the list of buses that are displayed then click bookme.
  • ➤ Select a seat number and mention your gender in the seat plan.
  • ➤ Put in the "Passenger Information" and "Contact Information".
  • ➤ Hit the checkout button to confirm your online bus booking.
